What is Runa?
Runa is an AI-powered running fitness app that helps you track runs, analyze performance, build streaks, and improve consistency with smart insights.
Is my data safe on Runa?
Yes. Your data is stored locally in your browser, giving you full control and strong privacy. You can also download a backup anytime.
Do I need an account to use Runa?
Yes. An account keeps your data secure and personalized, ensuring it stays separate from other users.
What can I track with Runa?
You can log your run distance, time, speed, notes, and view detailed stats, analytics, and progress over time.
Can I edit or delete my runs?
Yes. All runs can be edited or deleted at any time, and your stats update instantly.
Does Runa support goals and streaks?
Yes. Runa helps you set weekly goals and track running streaks to stay motivated and consistent.
What are AI Insights?
AI Insights analyze your running data and provide personalized tips, feedback, and training suggestions to help you improve.
Can I see analytics and charts?
Yes. Runa offers easy-to-read charts, heatmaps, and performance trends to track your progress visually.
Can I back up my data?
Yes. You can download your data as a backup file and restore it whenever needed.
Will Runa support cloud backup in the future?
Yes. Optional cloud backup is planned, allowing secure cross-device access while keeping you in control of your data.
Does Runa work on mobile devices?
Yes. Runa is fully responsive and works smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
How can I give feedback?
You can share feedback directly inside the app to help improve future updates.
